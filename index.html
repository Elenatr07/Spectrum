Products

  • TCM SYSTEM

    TECHNOLOGICAL CONNECTION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

    TCM system is designed to automate the process of technological connection of consumers to the networks of electric grid companies. It provides the ability to users to register for technological connection, form technical conditions, register technological connection agreement, calculate the cost, register the Act on the fulfilment of technical conditions and register the Act on the implementation of technological connection.

    Contact Us

  • TRAM SYSTEM

    TECHNICAL REPAIRS AND MAINTENANCE SYSTEM

    TRAM system is designed to automate the processes of creating production programs and scheduling maintenance and repair of energy companies. It aims to help organizations prevent emergencies caused by untimely maintenance of electrical equipment. The system monitors equipment status, systemises the spare parts and tracks their warranties, as well as helps to form workload sheets, and executive and reporting documentation.

    Contact Us

  • WTO APPLICATION

    WORKING TIME OPTIMIZATION APPLICATION

    The WTO application is designed to optimize the working time of installation and repair teams by quickly changing tasks. It enables organisations to eliminate the double recording of information on paper media with subsequent transfer to the accounting system.

    Contact Us

  • EPTL SYSTEM

    ELECTRIC POWER TRANSMISSION LINES SYSTEM

    EPT system is designed to optimize electric power transmission lines. It allows performing automatic placement of cable-supporting structures along the route based on the specified profile parameter. It also calculates wire sag curves and mounting sag, determines the normative and design loads on the supports and calculates the rate of land acquisition for construction and protected zones.

    Contact Us

  • TM SYSTEM

    TRANSFORMER MONITORING SYSTEM

    TM system is designed to optimise the transformer operation and timely detection of possible hidden defects. It monitors the performance of transformers, including analytics of gas generation and its influence on the state of the transformer, fixes and analyzes the nominal and peak load, calculates the overload mode, and generates analytics based on the received data. Integration of the system includes software, design, system installation and commissioning.

    Contact Us
